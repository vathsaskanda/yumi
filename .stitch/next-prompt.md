**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Color Palette: Strictly Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF), and soft Cream (#F5F2EA).
- Typography: Bodoni Moda (elegant serif) for headlines, Manrope (clean sans-serif) for body and labels.
- Borders and Corner Radius: Sharp 0px corners (rounded-none) for all components.

1. **Header**: Sticky minimalist bar with logo.png, brand name "YUMI", and "Shop" navigation link.

4. **Product Catalog**: Responsive grid displaying 6 nightwear products. Each product card has a clean white background, the product name, a price of "999 Rs", and a minimalist "Add to Cart" button.
5. **Testimonials**: Clean grid showing 2 customer reviews with 5-star ratings on a cream background.
6. **Footer**: Stark black footer with white text, including "Based in Mangaluru | PAN India Delivery", contact info, and Instagram handle `@yumi_dxb`.
